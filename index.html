I am a very special dog and I love my family very much! <3

Hi! My name is Dvash (which means "דבש" or Honey in Hebrew).
I’m an affectionate pitbull and almost 3 years old.
I love playing with everyone—kids, dogs, and even cats!
I grew up with cats, so I’ve always been friendly,
but I know how to stand up for myself if someone tries to hurt me.
I don’t really bark or growl; I like to talk with my eyes instead.
Long walks, running, and jumping are some of my favorite things to do.
I also love playing with all kinds of toys like balls and ropes.
I always push myself to the limit when I’m chewing toys,
so my humans buy me really tough ones like BIONIC and the black KONG toys,
or big thick ropes that don’t break easily.
